Frequently Asked Questioned ?

How long will it take to learn Quran Tajweed?

Learning Tajweed (correct pronunciation) is important for every Muslim. It could be a unique task for beginners, especially those whose first language is not Arabic. But regular practicing makes the noble mission easier. Although it differs from person to person, constant learning can help to achieve the task within 6 months.

Can I memorize Quran online?

“Samjho Quran” provides you the great opportunity to memorize the Book of guidance online from the comfort of your home. The one-to-one personal attention of qualified teachers and flexible schedule with regular classes make the whole noble process convenient and easier.

What is the importance of Qaida?

In the beginning, a Muslim must learn the Qaida to recite the Glorious Quran accurately. A Qaida is a collection of Arabic language alphabets that carries the rules of pronouncing the Quranic words accurately.

How can I teach myself Quran?

The holy Quran is a divine book of Allah which is in the Arabic language. But still, if an Arab wants to recite the Quran with tajweed, he will also need an authentic teacher to learn the Quran. So every Muslim must invest some time and start learning Quran from an expert teacher.
